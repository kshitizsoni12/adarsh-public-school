About Us

Adarsh Public School is a co-educational primary institution nestled in the heart of rural Chhattisgarh. We educate children from Nursery to Grade 8 with a focus on creativity, care, and value-based learning.

Our mission is to deliver affordable, high-quality education in a safe and nurturing environment—empowering every child in our village and nearby areas to dream, learn, and grow.
